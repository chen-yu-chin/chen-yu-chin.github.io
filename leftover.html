The space was set up with an armchair and a ladder, two microphones were placed beside them, alongwith several handmade contact microphones on the armchair. The performer climbed the ladder and then threw themself onto the armchair, the impact was captured in sound every time the performer jumped off. The recordings kept on replaying and evolving, overlapping with the feedback from the microphones, creating a scene of absurdity and chaos.
