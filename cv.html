Yuchin Chen    陳 瑜 璟



Yuchin Chen is a Taiwanese multimedia artist, currently based in Berlin. Yuchin's works deal with the senses of harmony/disharmony and association/disassociation between the time, space and self, often from the perspective of mediation, translation, and listening to explore new forms of understanding and perceiving the environment around us.
