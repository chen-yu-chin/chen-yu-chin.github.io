“Notes on floating” is an experimental writing project that unfolds around the meanings, bodily experiences, and metaphors of “floating”. Beginning from everyday observations, personal experiences, and island mythologies, “floating” here is not simply suspension or weightlessness, but a state of in-betweenness and openness. Through the rhythmic compositions of illustrations, images, and text, the project seeks to capture the resonance and fragmentation of language, depicting “floating” as a transient condition between rooting and uprooting, soil and water, myth and reality.
