YUCHIN CHEN



Born in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, currently based in Berlin, Germany. Yuchin draws inspiration from senses of harmony / disharmony and association / disassociation between the self and the world. Through her interest in and practice of bodily perception and integration of space into the creative process, she further draws attention to how the self experiences and perceive time and space, and how they shape our sense of physical presence and embodiment, or vice versa.
