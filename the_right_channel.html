“the Right channel” is a proposal of Anti-Media Listening, through modifications of an everyday use earphone. The earphone is covered with stickers displaying images, logos, sceenshots of mineral extraction sites, manufacture industry, brands and news related to the mass production of this earphone.

Without a source input, the earphone sculpture does not impose any sound or intention upon the user, but invites the listners to listen to the multi-layered mediations of media in silence.
